Visit Thrissur

Experience the cultural capital of Kerala, known for its rich heritage, stunning temples, and breathtaking waterfalls.

Top three things to do in Thrissur

Explore Vadakkunnathan Temple

A magnificent ancient Shiva temple, famous for its stunning Kerala-style architecture and mural paintings, and the grand Thrissur Pooram festival.

Witness the beauty of Athirapilly Waterfalls

Known as the "Niagara of India," Athirapilly Waterfalls offers a mesmerizing sight with its cascading waters amidst lush greenery.

Relax at Snehatheeram Beach

A serene beach with golden sands, perfect for enjoying a peaceful sunset and fresh seafood, ideal for family outings.
